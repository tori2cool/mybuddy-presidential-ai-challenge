Our Journey with MyBuddy: From a Preschool Dream to an AI-Powered Future of Learning
It all began with our daughter, Cali, who at just four years old in preschool declared her dream of becoming a teacher. Inspired by our family’s microgreens and hydroponics hobby, we teamed up to help her launch a monthly “Microgreen Mastery” class at her school. Over 10 days of 45-minute sessions, Cali guided her classmates through planting, nurturing, and harvesting their own trays, culminating in take-home projects that families harvested together. The kids loved it so much that every single one begged for more. This sparked an evolution: We wove in sustainable living lessons, teaching them to plant seeds from everyday foods, regrow green onions, and explore vegetable gardening. These chats with Cali about “what else can we teach?” often circled back to the life wisdom I’d share with friends, core values like respect, loyalty, and perseverance. She wondered why schools didn’t include that too.
Fast-forward a few years: Our homeschooling experiments with these ideas gained momentum just as breakthrough AI tools emerged, democratizing app development without the massive costs of traditional coding. Enter MyBuddy, our AI-driven homeschool companion app. Born from Cali’s passion and our shared vision, it’s reimagining education not just the ABCs and 123s of traditional curricula, but a holistic system that truly knows the child. It adapts lessons to their unique interests, learning style, and pace, blending academic subjects with essential life skills. Think brushing teeth with technique videos, gentle wake-up routines for a calm start to the day, balanced nutrition trackers, fun exercise challenges, and a healthy mix of focused work and joyful play. No more one-size-fits-all drudgery; instead, an enlightened path that keeps kids excited about their growth, evolving dreams, and boundless potential.
At its heart, MyBuddy ditches rigid grades for a flexible leveling system. You advance as fast or slow as feels right, always chasing your best self. It’s more than lessons; it’s a lifelong ally, from preschool explorers to college-bound trailblazers, evolving into tools like career trade simulations, personal coaching, fitness guidance, and health nudges.
Where We Stand Today: A Solid Foundation
Our current beta version is a delightful, intuitive app with a TikTok-inspired home feed scrolling through uplifting positive affirmations (with easy tools to create your own). Dive deeper, and you’ll find:
* Flashcard Academy: Bite-sized, interactive lessons in math, science, reading, and history.
* Outdoor Adventures: Nature-based activities to spark curiosity and get kids moving.
* Chores Hub: Gamified tasks that build responsibility with rewards and tips.
* Profile Portal: A personal dashboard to track progress and set mini-goals.
It’s simple, safe, and already sparking joy in early testers. It’s fully COPPA-compliant with minimal data collection, verifiable parental consent for any interactions, and privacy-by-design to protect young users. But this is just the launchpad.
